LISA MOSES : : INTERDISCIPLINARY ARTIST + DESIGNER

lisa moses is an interdisciplinary artist and designer in denver, colorado. she currently teaches a variety of classes and develops curriculum at Rocky Mountain College of Art + Design. her freelance work is diverse, but she especially loves creating handmade and environmental graphics. when she is not in the studio, she is building low-income housing projects with Habitat for Humanity. the natural environment inspires her endlessly, as does nanotechnology, quantum theory and the structure of sound.
